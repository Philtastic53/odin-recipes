Lasagna

This page is dedicated to creating a delicious lasagna. The recipe will simply list the ingredients for the recipe for individual use. The recipe can be altered to what fit personal tastes. Remember to use clean cookware and fresh ingredients.

Below is the list of ingredients followed by the directions.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow) , diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chpped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (preferably flrat leaf), packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)
  • salt

Cooking Steps

  1. 1/2 pount dry lasagna noodles (requires 9 lasagna noodles - unbroken)
  2. 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  3. 1 1/2 pounds (24 ounces) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  4. 1/4 pound (4 ounces) freshley grated Parmesan cheese
