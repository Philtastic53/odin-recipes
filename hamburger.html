Hamburger

The secret to an incredibly juicy burger patty is the addition of crushed crackers or panko breadcrumbs that hold the natural fat and juices. With that said, we will show you the way.

Below is the list of ingredients followed by the directions.

Ingredients

  • Ground chuck - preferably 80 lean/20 fat ratio for the juiciest burgers.
  • Crushed crackers or Pank bread crumbs
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Seasonings: Salt, garlic powder, onion powder, pepper, or other spices of choice

Cooking Steps

  1. Add ground beef, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire sauce, milk, and spices to mixing bowl
  2. Use your hands to thoroughly combine until the mixture is very smooth
  3. Press the meat down in the bowl, into an even disk
  4. WIth a knife, cut and divide the hambuger patty mixture into 6 - 1/3 pound grill or skillet patties, or 12 thin griddle patties
  5. Set out baking sheet, lined with wax paper or foil, to hold the patties.
  6. Press firmly on the patties to your desired thickness
  7. Place patties onto the baking sheet
  8. Preheat grill or skillet to medium heat, approximately 350 - 400 degrees F
  9. When ready, stick patties in grill/skillet
